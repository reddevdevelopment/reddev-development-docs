## REDDEV Advanced Admin Menu

A modern all-in-one FiveM administration system built for QBCore, QBox, and ESX servers.

***

### Features

* Player moderation tools
* Vehicle management
* Staff utilities
* Screenshot system
* Logs and monitoring
* ACE permission support
* txAdmin compatibility
* Framework auto detection
* Discord logging
* Staff modes
* Noclip
* Spectate
* Bring / Goto
* Revive / Heal
* Inventory management

***

### Supported Frameworks

* QBCore
* QBox
* ESX

***

### Recommended Dependencies

* ox\_lib
* oxmysql
* screenshot-basic

***

### Notes

The REDDEV Advanced Admin Menu is actively developed and updated regularly.
